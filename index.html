04 · Product investigation

HealthConnect — Telemedicine & EHR Integration

Systems coordination across 50+ hospitals · implementation planning · cross-functional execution

The challenge

Fragmented patient data systems, disconnected telemedicine infrastructure, and misaligned stakeholder incentives across a healthcare network. No single owner. No clear path.

The real product problem wasn't technical — it was coordination. Success required treating communication and trust as core product features.

How I approached it

  • Mapped stakeholder power vs interest across clinical, IT, compliance, and admin
  • Designed communication architecture as a product layer, not an afterthought
  • Structured a 12-week phased rollout with adoption checkpoints
  • Analyzed operational and compliance risks per phase
  • Balanced scalability, usability, and regulatory constraints simultaneously

06 · UX sprint

Temu UX Sprint — Cognitive Overload and the Trust Deficit

Design sprint · trust vs engagement tradeoffs · purchase confidence

The strategic tension

Temu optimized engagement aggressively — at the direct expense of usability, trust signals, and purchase clarity. A classic product tradeoff: short-term metrics vs sustainable conversion.

Engagement without clarity creates friction at the point of decision-making — where it matters most.

Proposed solution — "Focus Mode"

  • Cleaner visual hierarchy separating utility from gamified browsing
  • Reduced overlays and interruption patterns
  • Price stability indicator to rebuild trust
  • Distraction-free checkout path

07 · Systems analysis

Chowdeck — Order Accuracy & Fulfillment Trust

Research-driven product investigation into customization failures and workflow accountability

The product question

Why were users receiving orders that didn't match what they selected? The surface problem was delivery inaccuracy. The real problem was a communication gap across the fulfillment workflow.

It wasn't a delivery failure — it was an accountability gap between restaurant confirmation and actual fulfillment.

Research foundation

  • 48-user survey on order accuracy experiences
  • Behavioral and support-ticket analysis
  • Root cause investigation across the fulfillment chain
  • Secondary reviews and pattern analysis

Solution direction

Real-Time Substitution Approval — a communication layer that brings the user back into the fulfillment loop before the order ships, reducing mismatches and rebuilding trust at the point of uncertainty.
